    About Me

    Hello, I'm Yoostink! Welcome to my commissions gallery - a place where I can showcase the artwork I commissioned.

    I like to commission art from artists whom I admire for their work, especially the ones who draw Lucina a lot. I love Lucina! I think she's a very pretty and strong-willed lady. Most of my commissions will feature her as the main subject. If you're a fan of Lucina too, please consider joining the 1000+ member Lucina Fan Club Discord server using the invite link: https://discord.gg/lucina

    Unfortunately, I don't plan on accepting follower requests on Twitter. I only use my Twitter account to follow and contact artists. My DMs are open, but if you pester me with frequent messages or requests, I will block you. :-(

    This is a series of commissioned artwork depicting Lucina dressed in the Hooters outfit.

    Hooters Lucina was an inside joke between me and an artist friend. I commissioned them to draw the first Hooters Lucina back in 2018. Now, I guess it's something we can all share. Also, it is not a fetish thing.

    I first started with sending Hooters Lucina requests to a handful of artists in 2021. I thought the idea was too weird and zany to be accepted, which is why I shotgunned requests hoping for just one to be accepted. To my surprise, all of them were accepted. After the first few were delivered, I decided to request more as I saw more artists opening up commissions - artists whom I really admire for their work. I then decided to keep this commission series going as a way to support my favorite artists. Plus, it's fun to see people's reactions.

    Yes, I do plan on ending the series. I will transition to commissioning Lucina in other outfits and settings but not as a series, and the frequency of requests will drop. I may revisit Hooters Lucina and request it here and there however.

    I am not a fan of the new Hooters uniform that was revealed in October 2021. My commission requests will only have references to the original outfit design. I also always give the artist the freedom to alter the outfit design and omit/modify the logo on the shirt.
